Available Services

    Aesthetics Services

      Chemical Peel is a technique used to improve the appearance of the skin on the face, neck or hands.

    • A chemical solution is applied to the skin that causes it to exfoliate and eventually peel off the new, regenerated skin is usually smoother and less wrinkled than the old skin. The new skin is also temporarily more sensitive to the sun. The chemical peels are brief procedures that require very little recovery time, although multiple treatments are usually necessary to achieve the best results.
    • Benefits for acne scars, aging skin, melasma, hyperpigmentation, crow’s feet, wrinkles, sagging skin, and sun damaged skin.

      Target audience is ethnicity skin tones specifically those with uneven skin tone due to hyperpigmentation.

      Microneedling is a minimally invasive procedure that involves superficial controlled puncturing of the skin at a set depth with fine needles that creates channels in the skin.

    • Overall skin texture improvement happens over time and uses the body’s wound healing response to form new structures in the skin like collagen and elastin. These lead to a more youthful appearance and can decrease the appearance of fine lines and textural irregularities. It is a 30-minute office visit and a topical anesthetic is used.
    • Benefitsfor acne scars, hyperpigmentation, melasma, scars, stretch marks.

      Target audience is ethnicity skin tones specifically those with uneven skin tone due to hyperpigmentation.

      Neurotoxins/Neuromodulators are wrinkle relaxing injections of botulinum toxin-commercially known as Botox, Xeomin, Dysport, Jeuveau-that are used to treat wrinkles, frown lines and crow's feet.

    • A minute amount of the neurotoxins is injected directly into the underlying muscle, causing it to relax and gradually smooth out the appearance of the overlying skin. The effects typically last three months. Also FDA-approved to treat excessive sweating.
    • Benefits for wrinkles, crow’s feet, aging skin, sagging skin, frown lines and forehead furrows, and excessive sweating.

      Target audience is all person wanting to smooth and diminish wrinkles

    I.V. Therapies

      IV Nutrition Therapy is a medical treatment by which sterile solutions of vitamins, minerals, and micronutrients are infused directly into the veins of the patient.

    • IV nutrition therapy bypasses digestion to provide quicker delivery to the cells that need it to replenish deficiencies or aid in healing as well as to improve the body’s state of health and overall well-being.
    • Additional benefits benefits include boosting immune function, decrease inflammation and pain, prevent dehydration, and prevent cell damage.

      Target audience is all person who have poor diet, fatigue, dehydration, acute illness, and adjunct to support certain health conditions.
