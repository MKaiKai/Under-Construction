    • What to expect at your visit

      IV Therapy

        At your initial consultation, your clinician will discuss your primary concern, review your medical history, necessary labs that will be either order or evaluate your most current labs, and your candidacy to determine the most appropriate IV therapy options.

      • Consulation length: Receiving an IV can take from 45 minutes to 2 hours, depending on treatment. You should be well hydrated and have eaten a full meal prior to the treatment.

        You are monitored the entire time for safety, but you’re free to read, browse your phone, or listen to music during your IV therapy sessions. 

        Payment: We do accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, Zelle, HSA, and FSA

      BHRT [Bio-identical Hormone Replacement Therapy]

      • Initial Consultation a comprehensive review of your health history, order comprehensive labs that include thyroid panel, cholesterol panel, metabolic panel, complete blood count, hormone levels, and go over the BHRT treatment plan.
      • Follow-up in 4 weeks: To review improvement of symptoms, and adjustment of treatment accordingly with consideration of any needed labs/or evaluation of pellet treatment
