      IV Nutritional Therapy

        At your initial consultation, your clinician can help you go over each of the available IV therapy options. They make recommendations according to your needs at the time of your visit, if you’re not already sure which option to choose.

        The process takes about an hour, and you get to sit comfortably as the nutrients passively flow into your vein. You are monitored the entire time for safety, but you’re free to read, browse your phone, or listen to music during your IV therapy sessions. 

        IV therapy is, in many ways, more effective than taking oral supplements that contain the same nutrients. Instead of going through your digestive tract, which doesn’t result in 100% absorption, the nutrients go directly into your bloodstream for full absorption. 

      • Consulation length: Receiving an IV can take from 45 minutes to 2 hours, depending on treatment. You should be well hydrated and have eaten a full meal prior to the treatment.

      BHRT [Bio-identical Hormone Replacement Therapy]

      • Initial Consultation a comprehensive review of your health history, order comprehensive labs that include thyroid panel, cholesterol panel, metabolic panel, complete blood count, hormone levels, and go over the BHRT treatment plan.

      • Follow-up in 2-4 weeks: review of blood hormone levels plus initiation of therapy. Adjustment of treatment accordingly.

      • Follow up in 3-6 months: review hormone labs and adjust your therapy accordingly. Further follow ups will be discussed at that time.
